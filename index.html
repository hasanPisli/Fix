  • How It Works
1

Find the Right Service

Browse our extensive categories or use the search function to find the perfect service for your needs. Each service provider has a detailed profile with ratings, reviews, and pricing information to help you make an informed decision.

2

Book Your Appointment

Select your preferred date and time from the technician's availability calendar. Provide details about your service needs and confirm your booking with a few simple steps. You'll receive instant confirmation and reminders as your appointment approaches.

3

Receive Professional Service

Your technician arrives on time, fully equipped to handle your service request. They'll perform the job professionally and efficiently. After completion, you'll have the opportunity to review the service and provide feedback.

4

Secure Payment

After satisfactory completion of the service, payment is processed securely through our platform. You can choose from multiple payment methods. FixU holds payment until you confirm the job is completed to your satisfaction.
